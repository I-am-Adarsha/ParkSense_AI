04 / 10
AI / ML Models

Intelligence Under the Hood

🧠

DBSCAN Spatial Clustering

Density-based clustering with haversine distance metric identifies geographical hotspots from raw lat/lng coordinates. Found 145 clusters.

📈

Congestion Impact Score

Weighted multi-factor model (0-100) combining: violation density, road type severity, vehicle weight, peak hour concentration, and junction proximity.

🎯

Enforcement Prioritization

Composite algorithm combining congestion score, enforcement gap (areas with low action rates), violation recency, and repeat offender density. 54 Urgent zones flagged.

⏰

Temporal Analysis

Time-series decomposition across hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly granularity. Powers optimal enforcement shift scheduling.
